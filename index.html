Experience Brazilian culture.

Have fun, get fit, and learn Capoeira with Mestre Rodriguinho.

Capoeira Senzala Australia is lead by Mestre Rodriguinho and is based in Canberra, Australia. Our main goals are to promote Capoeira and Brazilian Culture and bring the benefits of playing Capoeira to everyone! We hold beginners courses and regular classes through out Canberra.
